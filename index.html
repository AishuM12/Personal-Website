About Us

Welcome to The Juice Laundry - your neighborhood spot for fresh, organic goodness right in the heart of Dupont Circle. We believe that clean, plant-powered fuel should be as delicious as it is nourishing. That’s why every smoothie, bowl, and booster we serve is made with 100% organic ingredients - always vegan, gluten-free, non-GMO, peanut-free, and soy-free.

We’re not just slinging smoothies - we’re on a mission to build a community around mindful eating, sustainability, and whole-body wellness. Whether you're stopping in post-yoga, grabbing a green glow on your lunch break, or refueling after a run - we’ve got your back (and your blender).
