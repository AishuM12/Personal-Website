Why I Made This

I’ve always wanted to document my travels - but never quite found the right way. Social media never felt like the place for it. I didn’t want to think about how a photo might come across, or whether something was “worth sharing.” I just wanted to be in the moment.

Sometimes, when you care less about capturing a memory, you actually end up living it more. And that’s what I’ve always wanted from travel. To be there fully. Not through a screen.

This website is simple. It’s just a space for me to collect what I’ve seen and felt - so I can remember. And maybe, one day, share it with close friends or my kids. If no one ever reads it, that’s fine too. It’s here for me.

This site isn’t about creating content. It’s just a record of the places that shaped me. A way to remember the things I might otherwise forget. The view from a bus window. The street I walked every day. The quiet mornings. The unexpected friendships.
