Why this website?

I’ve never been a fan of social media. The constant sharing, the pressure to keep up – it never felt like me. But even with that, I’ve always had this quiet desire to document my travels. To gather the little moments, the places, the people, and how it all made me feel.

For years, I kept telling myself I’d make something – maybe a journal, maybe a website. I just never got around to it. Other things always took up space.

This assignment gave me a reason to finally begin. And I want to name that, because inspiration shows up in unexpected ways. It’s funny how something that starts as coursework can spark something so personal. So here I am, starting this space not for the internet, but for myself. And maybe one day, for my kids.
